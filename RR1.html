Garden RR Project

In the beginning . . there was dirt. And some weeds. I came up with a basic track plan: a loop with a cut through (so I could change the direction of the trains) and a station with a siding. I also wanted to be able to bring the trains up to the sliding glass door in the patio. First, I built a wooden shed to store my rollong stock in, seen below without the roof

The shed area was my starting point. Here you can see 3 track lanes leading into it.

Before starting construction, I bought these gondola cars. In one car I would load 1ft sections of straight track, and in the other I kept track laying supplies and a beer. This way I could move everything along with me and it worked out great!
