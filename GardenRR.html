In a way, the garden RR project started back in the 80's, when my grandmother bought me my first LGB starter set. Over the years I slowly added pieces to the RR, buildings, engines, rolling stock etc. In Feb of 2014, I pulled it all out of my parents attic where it had all been sitting for about 20 years and decided it was time to put it where it belongs, outside!
